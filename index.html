Hierarchical Diagram Builder

Diagram of a recursive binary structure. Max two children per node.

Recursive division of a rectangle, inspired by treemaps, character decomposition and Metro design. Only terminal nodes (leaves) are displayed, unlike Binary Tree and Tabulator which show all levels. Use [...] for hierarchy, spaces as separators, "..." or «...» to group tokens. Even numbers of children per group (2, 4, 6...) produce the most balanced layouts.

Hierarchical data rendered as a merged-cell table. Same input as Binary Tree.
